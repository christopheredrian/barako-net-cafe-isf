Target Audience

Our target audience are primarily college students of Saint Louis University, their age ranges from 16-25, male and female. Not all of the students have their own computers and internet connection at their respective homes, so it is our primary goal to serve these types students with their computer, printing and scanning needs. More than fifty percent of the business profit will most likely come from this part of the business.

We also serve students who have their own laptops, they can use our special room which houses 20 LAN ports. For those without LAN ports the can use our wifi which is free of charge if they order anthing from the stores' cafe.

As provided by other internet cafe's around the vicinity, we also provide gaming rigs for gamers which houses the popular online games of the era.

Our cafe includes food and drinks which are served as requested by the customers who are anywhere around the internet cafe - dining area, LAN area and computer shop area.
